The Best Movies According to Cad

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wolf of Wall Street

I love the sales environment, the financial markets and the ambition of Jordan Belfort. Also, it teaches a great lesson by showing how everithing falls apart when you're doing things just to make money, with no concern of generating value to the society.

The Big Short

Great movie about capital markets. Simply love the jokes and the memes.

The Founder

Can't get enough movies about entrepreneurship.
